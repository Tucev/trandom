What is Cloudyama

1. The meaning of Cloudyama

This site is hosting on Google Appengine, which is the cloud computing platform. So we decided to take the "Cloud" from that for we believe that in the future, more website and services will be running in cloud computing platforms.

Because of the university of one of our founders is in the mountains in Japan, we took the "mountain". And create the concept of "could and mountain", and also because of mountain in Japanese is "Yama", we made the website called "Cloudyama"
